About me

Lufe Pantonial

I’m a website developer with nearly 8 years of hands-on experience building high-performing, conversion-focused websites. I specialize in WordPress, Shopify, GoHighLevel (GHL), ClickFunnels, and Webflow, delivering both custom builds and platform-optimized solutions. For WordPress development, I work extensively with Elementor, Divi, and Oxygen, allowing me to create flexible, scalable, and easy-to-manage websites. I’m also highly comfortable with custom development using HTML, PHP, JavaScript, and CSS, enabling me to build advanced functionality beyond standard page-builder limitations. I can accurately translate Figma designs into responsive, pixel-perfect websites and collaborate efficiently with designers and teams. My focus is always on clean code, performance optimization, and building solutions that align with real business goals.
